# Analysis of: MN41_p2

atha kho sāleyyakā brāhmaṇagahapatikā yena bhagavā ten'upasaṅkamiṃsu, upasaṅkamitvā app'ekacce bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekam'antaṃ nisīdiṃsu, app'ekacce bhagavatā saddhiṃ sammodiṃsu, sammodanīyaṃ kathaṃ sāraṇīyaṃ vītisāretvā ekam'antaṃ nisīdiṃsu, app'ekacce yena bhagavā ten'añjaliṃ paṇāmetvā ekam'antaṃ nisīdiṃsu, app'ekacce bhagavato santike nāmagottaṃ sāvetvā ekam'antaṃ nisīdiṃsu, app'ekacce tuṇhībhūtā ekam'antaṃ nisīdiṃsu. ekam'antaṃ nisinnā kho sāleyyakā brāhmaṇagahapatikā bhagavantaṃ etadavocuṃ, ko nu kho, bho gotama, hetu, ko paccayo, yena m'idh'ekacce sattā kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapajjanti? ko pana, bho gotama, hetu, ko paccayo, yena m'idh'ekacce sattā kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā sugatiṃ saggaṃ lokaṃ upapajjantī'ti?

### English Translation

**Translation:** Then the brahmin householders of Sālā went to the Blessed One. Having approached, some bowed to the Blessed One and sat down to one side. Some exchanged greetings with the Blessed One, and after exchanging polite and friendly conversation, sat down to one side. Some, raising their joined palms in reverence toward the Blessed One, sat down to one side. Some announced their name and clan in the presence of the Blessed One and sat down to one side. Some kept silent and sat down to one side. Sitting to one side, the brahmins and householders of Sālā said to the Blessed One: 'What is the cause, Master Gotama, what is the reason why some beings here, with the breakup of the body, after death, are reborn in a state of misery, a bad destination, ruin, purgatory?' But what, Master Gotama, is the cause, what is the reason, why some beings here, with the breakup of the body, after death, are reborn in a happy destination, in a heavenly world?

**Literal Translation:** Then indeed the Sālā-belonging brahmin-householders where the Blessed One was there approached. Having approached, some the Blessed One having bowed to one side sat down, some with the Blessed One together exchanged greetings, greeting-worthy talk memorable having completed to one side sat down, some where the Blessed One was there joined-palms having raised to one side sat down, some of the Blessed One in the presence name-and-clan having announced to one side sat down, some became-silent to one side sat down. to one side seated indeed Sālā-dwellers brahmins-and-householders to Blessed-One this said: what indeed cause Master Gotama what reason by-which here some beings of-body with-breakup after death state-of-misery bad-destination ruin purgatory are-reborn? What indeed, dear Gotama, cause, what reason, by which here some beings body's breaking-up beyond death good-destination heaven world arise?
