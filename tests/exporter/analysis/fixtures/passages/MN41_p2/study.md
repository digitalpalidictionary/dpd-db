# Analysis of: MN41_p2

atha kho sāleyyakā brāhmaṇagahapatikā yena bhagavā ten'upasaṅkamiṃsu, upasaṅkamitvā app'ekacce bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekam'antaṃ nisīdiṃsu, app'ekacce bhagavatā saddhiṃ sammodiṃsu, sammodanīyaṃ kathaṃ sāraṇīyaṃ vītisāretvā ekam'antaṃ nisīdiṃsu, app'ekacce yena bhagavā ten'añjaliṃ paṇāmetvā ekam'antaṃ nisīdiṃsu, app'ekacce bhagavato santike nāmagottaṃ sāvetvā ekam'antaṃ nisīdiṃsu, app'ekacce tuṇhībhūtā ekam'antaṃ nisīdiṃsu. ekam'antaṃ nisinnā kho sāleyyakā brāhmaṇagahapatikā bhagavantaṃ etadavocuṃ, ko nu kho, bho gotama, hetu, ko paccayo, yena m'idh'ekacce sattā kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapajjanti? ko pana, bho gotama, hetu, ko paccayo, yena m'idh'ekacce sattā kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā sugatiṃ saggaṃ lokaṃ upapajjantī'ti?

### English Translation

**Translation:** Then the brahmin householders of Sālā went to the Blessed One. Having approached, some bowed to the Blessed One and sat down to one side. Some exchanged greetings with the Blessed One, and after sharing pleasant and memorable talk, sat down to one side. Some saluted the Blessed One with joined palms and sat down to one side. Some announced their name and clan in the presence of the Blessed One and sat down to one side. Some kept silent and sat down to one side. Sitting to one side, the brahmin householders of Sālā said to the Blessed One: 'What is the cause, Master Gotama, what is the reason, why some beings here, with the breakup of the body, after death, are reborn in a state of loss, a bad destination, ruin, hell?' But what is the cause, Master Gotama, what is the reason, why some beings here, with the breakup of the body, after death, are reborn in a happy destination, in a heavenly world?

**Literal Translation:** Then indeed the Sālā-inhabiting brahmin-householders where the Blessed One was there approached, having approached, some the Blessed One having bowed to one side sat down, some with the Blessed One together exchanged greetings, polite talk memorable having finished to one side sat down, some where the Blessed One was there palms having saluted to one side sat down, some of the Blessed One in presence name-clan having announced to one side sat down, some silent-become to one side sat down. To one side sitting indeed Sālā-inhabiting brahmin-householders to the Blessed One this said, what indeed indeed, master Gotama, cause, what reason, by which here some beings of body breakup after death state of loss bad destination ruin hell are reborn? what but, Master Gotama, cause, what reason, by which here some
