# Analysis of: TH215

paṇṇavīsativassāni,
yato pabbajito ahaṃ,
accharā-saṅghāta-mattam'pi,
cetosantim'anajjhagaṃ.

### English Translation

**Translation:** It is twenty-five years since I went forth, yet I have not attained even a moment's peace of mind.

**Literal Translation:** Twenty-five years [it is], since gone forth I [am], even a finger-snap-measure, peace of mind I did not reach.

### Variants
