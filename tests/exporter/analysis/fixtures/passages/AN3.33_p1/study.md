# Analysis of: AN3.33_p1

atha kho āyasmā sāriputto yena bhagavā ten'upasaṅkami, upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekam'antaṃ nisīdi. ekam'antaṃ nisinnaṃ kho āyasmantaṃ sāriputtaṃ bhagavā etadavoca, saṃkhittena'pi kho ahaṃ, sāriputta, dhammaṃ deseyyaṃ, vitthārena'pi kho ahaṃ, sāriputta, dhammaṃ deseyyaṃ, saṃkhittavitthārena'pi kho ahaṃ, sāriputta, dhammaṃ deseyyaṃ, aññātāro ca dullabhā'ti. etassa, bhagavā, kālo, etassa, sugata, kālo yaṃ bhagavā saṃkhittena'pi dhammaṃ deseyya, vitthārena'pi dhammaṃ deseyya, saṃkhittavitthārena'pi dhammaṃ deseyya. bhavissan'ti dhammassa aññātāro'ti.

### English Translation

**Translation:** Then the Venerable Sāriputta approached the Blessed One. Having approached and paid homage to the Blessed One, he sat down to one side. As the Venerable Sāriputta was sitting to one side, the Blessed One said to him: "Sāriputta, I could teach the Dhamma in brief, Sāriputta, I could teach the Dhamma in detail, or, Sāriputta, I could teach the Dhamma both in brief and in detail, but those who understand are hard to find." Now is the time for this, Blessed One, now is the time for this, Sublime One, that the Blessed One should teach the Dhamma in brief, teach the Dhamma in detail, and teach the Dhamma both in brief and in detail. There will be those who understand the Dhamma.

**Literal Translation:** Then indeed the venerable Sāriputta where the Blessed One there approached, having approached the Blessed One having paid homage to one side sat down. To one side seated indeed to the venerable Sāriputta the Blessed One this said, in brief also indeed I, Sāriputta, Dhamma would teach, in detail also indeed I, Sāriputta, Dhamma would teach, in brief and detail also indeed I, Sāriputta, Dhamma would teach, understanders and are rare. Of this, Blessed One, [is] the time, of this, Sublime One, [is] the time, which the Blessed One in brief even the Dhamma should teach, in detail even the Dhamma should teach, in brief and detail even the Dhamma should teach. There will be of the Dhamma understanders.
