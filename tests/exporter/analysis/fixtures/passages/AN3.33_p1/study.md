# Analysis of: AN3.33_p1

atha kho āyasmā sāriputto yena bhagavā ten'upasaṅkami, upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekam'antaṃ nisīdi. ekam'antaṃ nisinnaṃ kho āyasmantaṃ sāriputtaṃ bhagavā etadavoca, saṃkhittena'pi kho ahaṃ, sāriputta, dhammaṃ deseyyaṃ, vitthārena'pi kho ahaṃ, sāriputta, dhammaṃ deseyyaṃ, saṃkhittavitthārena'pi kho ahaṃ, sāriputta, dhammaṃ deseyyaṃ, aññātāro ca dullabhā'ti. etassa, bhagavā, kālo, etassa, sugata, kālo yaṃ bhagavā saṃkhittena'pi dhammaṃ deseyya, vitthārena'pi dhammaṃ deseyya, saṃkhittavitthārena'pi dhammaṃ deseyya. bhavissan'ti dhammassa aññātāro'ti.

### English Translation

**Translation:** Then the Venerable Sāriputta approached the Blessed One, and having approached, he bowed to the Blessed One and sat down to one side. The Blessed One said this to the Venerable Sāriputta who was sitting to one side: 'I could teach the Dhamma in brief, Sāriputta, I could teach the Dhamma in detail, I could teach the Dhamma both in brief and in detail, but those who understand are hard to find.' 'Now is the time for this, Blessed One! Now is the time for this, Sublime One! Let the Blessed One teach the Dhamma in brief, let him teach the Dhamma in detail, let him teach the Dhamma both in brief and in detail. There will be those who understand the Dhamma!'

**Literal Translation:** Then indeed Venerable Sāriputta where the Blessed One was there approached, having approached the Blessed One having bowed to one side sat down. To the sitting to one side indeed Venerable Sāriputta the Blessed One this said: 'in brief also indeed I, Sāriputta, Dhamma could teach, in detail also indeed I, Sāriputta, Dhamma could teach, in brief and detail also indeed I, Sāriputta, Dhamma could teach, understanders and are hard to find.' 'Of this, Blessed One, time, of this, Sublime One, time, that the Blessed One in brief also Dhamma should teach, in detail also Dhamma should teach, in brief and detail also Dhamma should teach. There will be of the Dhamma understanders.'
