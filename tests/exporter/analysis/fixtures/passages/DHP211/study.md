# Analysis of: DHP211

tasmā piyaṃ na kayirātha,
piy'āpāyo hi pāpako,
ganthā tesaṃ na vijjanti,
yesaṃ n'atthi piy'āppiyaṃ.

### English Translation

**Translation:** Therefore, one should not hold anything dear, for separation from the beloved is painful. No bonds exist for those to whom nothing is dear or not dear.

**Literal Translation:** Therefore dear not one should make, separation from dear for evil, bonds of them not are found, to whom not is dear-undear.
